About: Dr. Francis Jesmar P. Montalbo (F. J. P. Montalbo) is a Filipino researcher, author, and Associate Professor who specializes in computer vision, biomedical signal processing, deep learning, data science, and image analysis. He completed his Doctorate in Information Technology with the highest honors at the Technological Institute of the Philippines-Manila, and has been actively contributing to Artificial Intelligence (AI) research, especially deep learning, through publications in reputable international journals and presentations at recognized conferences. Currently, Dr. Montalbo serves as an Associate Professor and Research Scientist at Batangas State University, The National Engineering University Philippines, where he mentors fellow researchers and fosters collaborative learning. He also serves as a peer reviewer for respected journals and conferences including Elsevier, Springer, ACM, Taylor & Francis, PLOS ONE, Wiley, IEEE, Frontiers Media, and Hindawi. Additionally, he contributes as an associate editor for a prominent local university-based journal in the Philippines.

About Me

Dr. Francis Jesmar P. Montalbo (F. J. P. Montalbo) is a Filipino researcher, author, and Associate Professor specializing in computer vision, biomedical signal processing, and deep learning.

He earned his **Doctorate in Information Technology** with the highest honors from the Technological Institute of the Philippines-Manila. He actively contributes to **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** research, particularly **deep learning**, with **publications in top-tier journals** and **conference presentations**.

Currently, Dr. Montalbo is an **Associate Professor and Research Scientist** at Batangas State University, mentoring researchers and fostering **collaborative innovation**. He is also a **peer reviewer** for leading journals like **Elsevier, Springer, ACM, IEEE, and Frontiers Media**.

Recognitions & Achievements

Dr. Montalbo is recognized in **Elsevier & Stanford’s Top 2% Most-Cited Scientists** (2019-2024), reflecting his **global impact in AI and data science**. His research contributions continue to shape the future of AI.

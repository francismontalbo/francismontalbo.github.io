Dr. Francis Jesmar P. Montalbo

Associate Professor | Research Scientist | AI & Deep Learning Specialist

Batangas State University
College of Informatics and Computing Sciences (CICS)

Dr. Montalbo completed his Doctorate in Information Technology with the highest honors at Technological Institute of the Philippines-Manila. He actively contributes to Artificial Intelligence (AI) research, particularly deep learning, through publications in top-tier international journals and conference presentations.

Currently, he serves as an Associate Professor & Research Scientist at Batangas State University, mentoring researchers and fostering collaborative learning. He is also a peer reviewer for Elsevier, Springer, ACM, Taylor & Francis, PLOS ONE, Wiley, IEEE, Frontiers Media, and Hindawi.

Additionally, Dr. Montalbo contributes as an Associate Editor for a university-based research journal in the Philippines.
